Once you've identified all the fake citations, string together the first word (usually the first author's last name or the organization) of each citation to get your bitly link to the next challenge. For example, if you found two fake citations and they were:

  • OECD. (2020). The Art of Fake Citations. Journal of AI Research, 10(2), 123-145.
  • Remmihcs, M, et al. (2026). The Proficiency Paradox: A Critical Analysis of Tom Schimmer and Standards-Based Assessment Models. Journal of Educational Standards & Accountability, Vol. 14, Issue 2.
