SD72 Pro D - AI (there is no) Escape

This workshop consists of a series of puzzles, lessons, and explorations around how artificial neurons laced together into neural networks and given a little attention and a transformer, can result in the magic of Large Language Models (LLMs) like Copilot, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and others.

The goal of the workshop is to give you a better understanding, conceptually, of how LLMs work, and maybe some ideas how you can use or teach about them in your classroom.

The workshop is designed so that you can use any single challenge, or all of them, in your classroom if you wanted to. If you are a student who has found their way here because your teacher shared this link with you, welcome!

Solving one challenge will lead to the next, in the form of a url that will take you to a page with instructions for the next challenge.
